Ramen Noodle Soup

Description

This soup is just very very good.. Can find ramen noodles at most supermarkets, or at Asian grocery stores

Ingredients

  • Vegetables broths
  • Ramen noodles
  • Soy sauce
  • Chili oil
  • Sesame oil
  • Onions

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan combine broth and noodles

    Cover and bring to a boil over high heat; stir to break up noodles

    Reduce heat to medium and add soy sauce, chili oil and ginger

    2. Simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes. Stir in sesame oil and garnish with green onions
